About me

I'm a second year Filmmaking student at UWE Bristol specialising in post-production. I'm experienced in editing drama/documentary student shorts and proficient with Premiere Pro and DaVinci Resolve. I'm looking to expand my portfolio of work, both long and short form, and am open to freelance jobs or work experience.

Christine (WIP)

An 8 minute expermental student short about the experiences of Christine Bott in the 70s. On this project I worked as script supervisor, DIT, and editor. This is a recent, unfinished cut.

A Living Document

A 10 minute documentary made for university exploring Document Studios, a space for creatives in Bristol. I was involved throughout all stages of production, though my primary role was editor and colourist.

Where the Stones Lay

A first year university film about the unlikely bond between a man and a school girl who meet on a beach. Again I worked as script supervisor, DIT, and editor.

The Decay of the Angel

A personal project created with a friend. This 2 minute experimental short was shot entirely in a whiteout studio. I worked assisting with camera work and lighting, and edited this final cut.
